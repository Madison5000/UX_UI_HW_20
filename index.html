The Case of the Savvy Traveler

The Local- A travel app

The Problem

Travelers face numerous options for planning the perfect trip, but are anxious about overplanning or missing out on the cool local spot that makes the whole trip feel worth it.

The Solution

The LOCAL puts travelers in touch with local in their destination who can recommend must-do’s and collaborate to help make the traveler “go beyond the guide book”.

My Role

UX Designer Researcher Tester

Tools Used

Miro, PPTX, Adobe InDesign, Adobe xD, Adobe Photoshop, Zoom, Survey Monkey

Research

Developed a proto-persona and hypothesised what we would discover. Used our research plan to guide in 8 one-on-one interviews and 40 surveys utilizing Survey Monkey.
